Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AD Build Advanced Procurement For General Trading (hereinafter, "we", "us", "our"), a company registered in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates, with offices at Building No. 412, 8th Street, Mussafah M9, Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates (hereinafter, the "Showroom") collects, uses, stores and protects personal information when you communicate with us through our official messaging channels on WhatsApp, Instagram and Facebook, or interact with advertisements that direct you to those channels (collectively, the "Service").

We are committed to processing your personal data in accordance with the UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on the Protection of Personal Data (PDPL), and where applicable, the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). By contacting us through any of the channels listed below, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Who is the data controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is AD Build Advanced Procurement For General Trading, contactable at info@adbuild.ae. For questions about this policy or to exercise your rights, please use that email.

2. Information we collect

When you message us on WhatsApp, Instagram or Facebook — including when you click on one of our ads and start a conversation — we receive the following information from Meta Platforms, Inc. ("Meta") and store it on our messaging system:

2.1 Information you provide

  • The content of messages you send us (text, images, voice notes, documents, location, contact cards).
  • Any product, pricing or service details you discuss.
  • Information you choose to share for sales follow-up, such as your name, delivery address, project requirements or budget.

2.2 Information from the messaging platform

  • Your public profile name and profile picture as shown on WhatsApp, Instagram or Facebook.
  • Your WhatsApp phone number (only for WhatsApp conversations) or your scoped Instagram/Facebook user identifier.
  • Timestamps of messages and read/delivery receipts.

2.3 Advertising attribution

  • When you start a conversation by clicking a "Click to WhatsApp", "Click to Messenger" or Instagram "Send Message" ad, Meta provides us with the ad reference: an ad identifier, the ad headline, the source URL, and a click identifier (e.g. ctwa_clid). This lets us route your enquiry to the section of the Showroom most relevant to the ad you saw.

2.4 Internal records we generate

  • Internal notes added by Showroom staff to track the conversation.
  • Conversation status (e.g. open, won, lost) and which employee handled the enquiry.
  • Aggregate metrics for the manager (number of conversations, response times, close rate).

We do not collect special categories of personal data (health, religion, biometric data) and we do not intentionally collect data from children under 18.

3. How we use your information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiry — answering questions about our products, prices, availability and arranging visits, quotations or deliveries.
  • To route the conversation to the right Showroom section (e.g. tiles, ceramics, tools) based on the ad you clicked or the topic of your message.
  • To follow up on open opportunities — sending you a courteous reminder if a conversation is left without a reply, in line with WhatsApp Business policies and the 24-hour customer-service window.
  • To improve our service and measure ad performance — analysing, in aggregate, which ads and sections perform best, and the close rate of enquiries. These analytics do not target individual customers.
  • To meet our legal obligations — such as record-keeping for tax, consumer-protection and accounting purposes.

The legal bases on which we rely are:

  • Performance of a contract or steps taken at your request prior to entering into a contract (replying to your sales enquiry).
  • Legitimate interest in running our business, tracking enquiries and measuring the performance of our advertising.
  • Consent, where required — for example, for marketing follow-ups outside the 24-hour service window via approved WhatsApp message templates.
  • Compliance with legal obligations under UAE law.

4. How we share your information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share it only with:

  • Meta Platforms, Inc. — as the operator of WhatsApp, Instagram and Facebook, Meta processes the messages you send to us in order to deliver them. Meta's own handling of your data is governed by its Privacy Policy and the WhatsApp Privacy Policy.
  • Our hosting and infrastructure providers, who store our messaging system on our behalf:
    • Railway Corp. (compute and database hosting). See Railway's privacy policy.
    • Cloudflare, Inc. (DNS and content delivery). See Cloudflare's privacy policy.
  • Authorised Showroom staff — employees of AD Build Advanced Procurement For General Trading who reply to your enquiries, on a need-to-know basis. Each employee has access only to conversations relevant to their section.
  • Authorities, when required by law, court order or a lawful request from a competent UAE authority.

5. Data storage, security and international transfers

Our messaging system runs on infrastructure operated by Railway Corp., which may store data in data centres located outside the United Arab Emirates, including in the United States. By contacting us through these channels, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred and processed outside the UAE. We take reasonable steps to ensure that any such transfer is protected by appropriate safeguards consistent with the UAE PDPL.

We protect your data with industry-standard measures, including TLS encryption in transit, access controls, role-based permissions inside our messaging system, and audit logs of staff actions.

6. Data retention

We retain conversation records and the personal data they contain for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes set out in this policy, and in any case for no longer than 24 months after the last interaction, unless a longer retention period is required by law (for example, for accounting or tax records).

7. Your rights

Subject to the UAE PDPL and any other applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Be informed of how your data is processed (this policy).
  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to erasure"), where this does not conflict with our legal obligations.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, including direct-marketing follow-ups.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent.
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Lodge a complaint with the UAE Data Office or another competent supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please email info@adbuild.ae. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law.

8. Cookies and similar technologies

This Privacy Policy covers our messaging Service. We do not set cookies on the messaging applications themselves (WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook) — those are operated by Meta. If you visit our website, any cookies used there are described in a separate Cookie Policy on that site.

9. Children's privacy

Our Service is not directed at, and we do not knowingly process personal data of, individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a child has shared personal data with us, please contact info@adbuild.ae and we will delete that data promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the date of the latest revision at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through our official channels.
